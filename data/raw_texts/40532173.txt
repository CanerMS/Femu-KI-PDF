 The cervical cancer reporting lexicon provides 
standardized consensus-based terms and definitions 
pertaining to the initial staging, follow-up, and treatment 
planning of cervical cancer.
 
 This lexicon represents a multidisciplinary and 
multiinstitutional collaboration involving participants 
from the SAR UOC DFP, the ESUR Female Pelvic Imaging 
Working Group, and the ASAR.
 
 The lexicon aims to promote consistent reporting of 
cervical cancer imaging, improve communication among 
multidisciplinary care team members, and enable 
meaningful comparisons across research investigations.
